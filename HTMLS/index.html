VitaLiquid Forte Plus
A premium multi-vitamin liquid supplement formulated for poultry and livestock. Supports immune function, growth performance, and reproductive efficiency.
FormOral Liquid Solution
Concentration500 ml / 1L / 5L
Target SpeciesPoultry, Cattle, Sheep
Dosage1 ml per 2L drinking water
Shelf Life24 months
StorageCool, dry place below 25°C
✓ Licensed 🔬 Lab-Tested 🐾 Vet-Approved
Usage by Species
Animal Applications
🐔
Poultry
  • Improves feed conversion ratio and growth rate
  • Supports egg production and shell quality
  • Boosts immune response during stress periods
  • Reduces mortality rate in broilers
🐄
Cattle
  • Enhances milk yield and quality in dairy cows
  • Supports reproductive performance and fertility
  • Maintains bone density and muscle strength
🐑
Sheep
  • Promotes healthy wool growth and quality
  • Supports lamb development and vitality
  • Boosts immunity during pregnancy
Key Benefits
Why Choose VitaLiquid Forte Plus?

Developed with leading veterinary nutritionists, VitaLiquid Forte Plus combines essential vitamins A, D3, E, and B-complex with trace minerals to cover the full spectrum of nutritional needs.

Our proprietary micro-encapsulation technology ensures maximum bioavailability and stability throughout the product's shelf life — even in challenging water conditions.

Our Story
Who We Are

VetGroup was founded in 2010 with a single mission: to manufacture world-class veterinary medicines that improve animal health, farm productivity, and food safety standards across the globe.

Our state-of-the-art GMP-certified production facility in Istanbul operates under strict international quality standards. We invest heavily in research and development to continuously improve our formulations.

From our headquarters, we serve distributors, veterinarians, and farmers in over 30 countries — providing not just products, but a complete technical support ecosystem.
